Our Story

Fifteen Years of Gilded Excellence

Founded in 2009 in London's Clerkenwell design district, Foilcraft began as a one-press studio with a single belief: that the physicality of print is irreplaceable.

Today we operate three specialist presses, employ twelve master craftspeople, and serve brands across six continents — but every piece still passes through human hands before it leaves our doors.

Philosophy

More Than Print

In a world of digital noise, a beautifully crafted physical piece commands silence and attention. We believe that the best design is felt as much as seen.

Every material we select, every die we commission, and every press run we approve is guided by one question: "Would this make someone stop and feel something?"

That commitment to emotional resonance is what separates a printed piece from a memorable one.
