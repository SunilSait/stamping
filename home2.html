Artwork Prep Guidelines

Pre-Press Manual · 2026
01 / Vector Format

Outlined Shapes Only

All files must be supplied in EPS, AI, or PDF vector formats. Every typeface, symbol, and line must be fully outlined. No raster images (PNG, JPG) can be converted to dies.

DO Expand appearance in Illustrator
02 / Line Weights

Minimum 1.0pt Thickness

To ensure crisp release of foil and clean embossing, all strokes and gaps must be a minimum of 1pt (approx. 0.35mm). Hairlines will fill in or fail to transfer.

DON'T Avoid thin script serif hairlines
03 / Color Specifying

100% Solid Black Plates

Set all die elements to 100% Solid Key Black (K=100) on separate layers labeled "Foil Stamping" or "Embossing". Do not include gradients, transparencies, or mid-tones.

DO Layer name separation in file
04 / Safe Margins

3mm Bleed & 4mm Safe Zone

Keep all text and essential details at least 4mm away from the crop marks. For die-cut items, extend any background foils or colors exactly 3mm past the cut line.

DO Include exact crop marks
