Packaging Project No. 042

The Nocturne Perfume Box

For a Parisian luxury perfumery, we created an outer sleeve box using 350gsm Gmund Color stock. We combined deep blind debossing for the nocturnal texture with mirror-perfect gold hot foil stamping for the branding shield.

  • Client: Nocturne Paris
  • Techniques: Blind Deboss + Gold Foil
  • Stock: Gmund Matte Charcoal

Stationery Project No. 089

The E&R Bespoke Suite

A bespoke luxury wedding invitation suite. We letterpress printed the classic script using custom mixed Pantone charcoal ink on extra-thick 600gsm cotton board, then applied hot rose gold foil gilding to the hand-beveled edges.

  • Client: Private Brief
  • Techniques: Letterpress + Rose Gold
  • Stock: Crane Lettra Cotton

Corporate Identity Project No. 115

The Vantara Business Cards

For an high-end architecture agency, we duplex-bonded a warm neutral board with deep slate grey board. The branding was blind debossed, and we cut a unique custom arched shape using a bespoke steel die sand colour.

  • Client: Vantara Architects
  • Techniques: Duplexing + Custom Die
  • Stock: Colorplan Slate / Sand
