Technique 05

Debossing

The inverse of embossing — pressing a design into the surface to create a concave impression. Ideal for monograms, logos and pattern backgrounds.

  • Combinable with foil stamping
  • Register precision to ±0.2mm
  • Available in soft and hard depths

Technique 06

Spot UV Coating

A glossy, high-contrast UV varnish applied precisely over selected areas. Creates a stunning visual contrast between matte and gloss surfaces.

  • Matte / gloss contrast effects
  • 3D raised UV also available
  • Pairs beautifully with foil

Eco-Craftsmanship

Sustainable Luxury

Luxury printing doesn't have to compromise the environment. Our hot foil stamping and embossing processes are engineered to meet the highest ecological standards.

Fully Recyclable Foils

Our hot foil stamping transfer layer is micro-thin (under 2.5 microns). Foil-stamped boards and papers are 100% repulpable and recyclable through normal local paper recycling programs.

Recycling Compatibility 100% Repulpable

FSC-Certified Stocks

We source exclusively from FSC-certified mills that practice sustainable forest management. We also offer 100% post-consumer recycled cotton and hemp boards.

Paper Source Quality FSC & Recycled Only

Non-Toxic Chemistry

Unlike traditional liquid print coatings, hot foil transfer is dry and VOC-free. Our inks are soy and vegetable-based, eliminating heavy metals and hazardous waste.

Emissions Level Zero VOCs / Solid Dry
